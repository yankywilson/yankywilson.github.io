I'm an independent threat intelligence researcher focused on phishing-to-RAT campaigns, rogue remote-management software (RMM) abuse, and the OPSEC tradecraft of commodity-tier criminal operators.

## What I work on

- **Rogue RMM tenant abuse** — ConnectWise ScreenConnect, AnyDesk, Atera, and the broader ecosystem of legitimate remote-management products being weaponized as RATs
- **ClickFix and similar social-engineering chains** — the human-in-the-loop layer that bypasses email security gateways
- **Initial Access Broker tradecraft** — operator OPSEC, infrastructure rotation, and kit-family fingerprinting
- **Network-layer detection** — signatures that work without TLS interception, focused on commodity malware kits

## How I work

- PCAP and behavioral analysis (Recorded Future Triage, Joe Sandbox, ANY.RUN)
- MSI / installer reverse engineering and tenant-build fingerprinting
- Multi-platform OSINT pivoting (VirusTotal, Censys, Validin, urlscan, MalwareBazaar, ThreatFox)
- Coordinated abuse outreach and disruption
- MITRE ATT&CK mapping for documented TTPs

## Working principles

- **Calibration over confidence** — I'd rather publish a report that says "OSINT cannot answer this" than overclaim attribution
- **Defang IOCs** — all published indicators are defanged for safe distribution
- **No operator-personal data** — research is about tradecraft and infrastructure, not doxxing
- **Open license** — research is published under MIT (code/rules) and CC BY 4.0 (writeups) for community use
